Who We Are

TULSEC is a CitySec group based out of Tulsa, OK. We loosely formed in 2017 as a periodic meetup to talk about all things Information Security. We wanted to be able to get away from corporate or vendor events and share ideas, learn together, and foster collaboration among members.

While we are mostly all Information Security professionals, this is not a professional society and we don't represent our employers at these meetups.

We have no membership requirements or dues.

We don't want to be a sales ground, we invite vendors to attend and speak, but we ask that presentations are as vendor-neutral as possible.





Our Meetups

We try our best to meet up the Third Thursday of Each Month and we typically meet at a local establishment that serves food and drinks.
